Khayyat Calcy — Account & Data Deletion

This action is permanent

Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed within 30 days:

  • Your account profile (name, email, photo)
  • All orders and piece tracking data
  • All bills and invoices
  • Company information (name, address, contact)
  • Party/shop records
  • Machine and katwar records
  • Model numbers and rates
  • Device registration data
  • Subscription and billing settings

How to request deletion

1

Contact us on WhatsApp

Send us a message with the email address associated with your Khayyat Calcy account.

2

We verify your identity

We'll confirm your account ownership by matching your request with your registered email.

3

Data is permanently deleted

Your account and all associated data will be deleted from our systems within 30 days.
